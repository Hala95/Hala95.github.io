Our story start from the much love of plants. We started the AYLOUL brand in 2018 as a way to send fresher plants &  flowers to loved ones faster and for less money than brick and mortar retail florists. 

While traditional providers were using antiquated technology: telephones, wire services and mail-in order forms to get gifts from one destination to another, we were mastering the art of growing the best plants and adding the science of an revolutionary online experience. Since that first plant was sold, we have continued to innovate, design, create and absolutely love what we do.

Let us be part of the gifting experience and we will show you what our promise is all about. In the mean time, take a look around, send that special someone a gift and let us know how we can continue to improve your experience.
